Abstract

The purpose of this vocabulary is to represent the information of the "Budget and budget execution" according to the municipality's economic management data. As an exercise on transparency in the municipalities that participate in this vocabulary, it gathers information on the munipality√© public accounting in its planning, the budget itself, as well as in its realization, that is the different budget executions. The scope of this vocabulary is focused on covering the representation of the minimal data required in the law 19/2013 from December 9th, on transparency, access to public information and good government, art. 8, referred to economic, budget and statistical information. Budget and budget execution of the current fiscal year are taken into account as well as information from previous years in order to be able to establish comparative analysis.
